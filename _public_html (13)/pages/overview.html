About Us

About Us

Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself

P. N. Institute, educates its students in the fields of Science, Commerce, Arts to discover and transmit knowledge and to make lasting contributions in our society through critical thinking and innovation. The core values of our teaching consists of four basic values: dignity, truthfulness, fairness and responsibility.

Vision

Our vision is for each child to develop a curiosity of learning, discover their interests and grow in their love of learning. We also desire to have strong families through parent support/fellowship and skills training.

Mission

P. N. Institute seeks to create a challenging learning environment that encourages high expectations for success through development-appropriate instruction that allows for individual differences and learning styles.

Motto

Our motto is "Linking learning to life." Fusing education with our life is the finest way to learn. Education is not only about studying the syllabus. It is a mix of physical, mental as well as social development.

  • Clothes Distribution for under privilege children and people in need

    • PNF has slum school for children who can’t afford school. It takes a big heart to shape little minds. PNF preaches education, Values, beliefs & habits to slum kids of Vadodara on every Sunday.
